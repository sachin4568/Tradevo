\# Tradevo



> AI-powered trading platform focused on reducing investment risk through personalized intelligence, behavioral learning, and AI-assisted decision support.



\---



\## Overview



Tradevo is a next-generation trading platform designed to help both beginner and experienced investors make informed investment decisions.



Unlike traditional trading platforms, Tradevo combines market data, AI-driven research, behavioral analysis, and a virtual trading simulator to help users learn, improve, and invest more confidently.



\---



\## Vision



To build an intelligent investment companion that empowers users to make data-driven investment decisions while continuously improving their investing skills.
